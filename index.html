Welcome!

When you open your inner self to Tango, and let it take your body, there will come the magical moment where you feel united with your partner through the dance. Then, you stop listening to the music, and you stop thinking about the steps, because you don’t need that any more: the music is already inside of you, it has become a part of your body and guides each one of your movements. Then, you feel one with your partner, the music and the universe, and everything flows harmonically.
